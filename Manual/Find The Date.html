On the Subject of Find The Dates

Have you ever wondered what day was December 25, 1862? Or if the 27th of June, 2491 is gonna be a Friday or a Saturday? Yeah, me neither...

Using the tables below, you must find out what day was the one on the module's display.

You need to get two numbers from Table 1 and Table 2: find the intersection between day and month in Table 1, then find the intersection between year and century in Table 2 and finally find the intersection between the two numbers you got from Table 1 and Table 2.

You can use the two arrows on the bottom to change day; to submit click on the correct day. Submitting the wrong day will cause in a strike. Find 3 dates to pass the module.

Some dates may not coincide with the original dates (like 16th century, which has different formules)
